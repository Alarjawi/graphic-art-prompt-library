# Brutalism

## Visual Logic

Brutalist graphic design is intentionally raw, heavy, direct, and anti-polished. Use it for cultural posters, experimental brands, music visuals, and editorial statements.

## Keywords

- raw layout
- heavy typography
- stark contrast
- exposed grid
- rough texture
- oversized type
- utilitarian composition
- monochrome with harsh accent
- anti-polished design

## Prompt Formula

```text
[format] for [subject], brutalist graphic design, raw utilitarian layout, oversized heavy typography, stark black white contrast with one harsh accent color, exposed grid, rough photocopy texture, intentionally imperfect spacing, no elegant ornament, no glossy mockup, no soft lifestyle aesthetic
```
