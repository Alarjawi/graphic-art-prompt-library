# De Stijl

## Visual Logic

De Stijl-inspired design is built from reduction, orthogonal structure, primary color blocks, black rules, and white space. Prompts should avoid pictorial detail and focus on balanced abstraction.

## Keywords

- orthogonal grid
- black horizontal and vertical lines
- primary red yellow blue
- white negative space
- pure abstraction
- balanced rectangles
- flat color
- minimal geometry

## Prompt Formula

```text
[format] for [subject], De Stijl-inspired graphic composition, orthogonal grid, black horizontal and vertical rules, primary red yellow blue blocks, white negative space, balanced rectangles, pure abstraction, flat color, no curves, no texture-heavy realism, no decorative illustration
```

## Example

```text
album cover for minimal electronic music, De Stijl-inspired graphic composition, orthogonal grid, black horizontal and vertical rules, primary red yellow blue blocks, white negative space, balanced rectangles, pure abstraction, flat color, no curves, no texture-heavy realism, no decorative illustration
```
