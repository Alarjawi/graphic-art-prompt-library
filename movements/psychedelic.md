# Psychedelic

## Visual Logic

Psychedelic graphic design suits music posters, festival identities, and expressive editorial visuals. Prompts should control the chaos with specific color, typography, and composition instructions.

## Keywords

- warped lettering
- vibrating color
- radial composition
- optical patterns
- surreal poster
- flowing shapes
- high saturation
- 1960s concert poster influence
- dense ornamental rhythm

## Prompt Formula

```text
[format] for [subject], psychedelic graphic poster, warped flowing lettering, saturated vibrating colors, radial composition, optical pattern background, surreal organic shapes, screenprint texture, energetic but readable layout, no photorealism, no bland modern minimalism, no muddy colors
```
