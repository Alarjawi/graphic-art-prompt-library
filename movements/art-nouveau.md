# Art Nouveau

## Visual Logic

Art Nouveau works well for posters, packaging, invitations, and ornamental editorial graphics. Prompts should emphasize flowing organic lines, botanical forms, decorative frames, and vintage lithographic softness.

## Keywords

- sinuous linework
- botanical ornament
- flowing curves
- elegant frame
- flat decorative illustration
- vintage lithographic poster
- muted natural palette
- hand-drawn lettering
- asymmetrical organic composition

## Prompt Formula

```text
[format] for [subject], Art Nouveau-inspired graphic illustration, sinuous botanical linework, flowing organic curves, elegant ornamental frame, muted natural palette, flat decorative forms, vintage lithographic poster texture, graceful hand-lettered title area, no photorealism, no modern corporate minimalism, no 3D
```

## Example

```text
tea packaging label for jasmine green tea, Art Nouveau-inspired graphic illustration, sinuous botanical linework, flowing organic curves, elegant ornamental frame, muted sage ivory and gold palette, flat decorative forms, vintage lithographic poster texture, graceful hand-lettered title area, no photorealism, no modern corporate minimalism, no 3D
```
