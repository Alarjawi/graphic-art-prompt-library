# Art Deco

## Visual Logic

Art Deco works for luxury posters, hospitality branding, packaging, and event graphics. Prompts should emphasize symmetry, geometric ornament, metallic accents, vertical elegance, and theatrical contrast.

## Keywords

- symmetrical composition
- stepped geometry
- sunburst motif
- metallic gold accents
- black and jewel tones
- elegant vertical layout
- luxury poster
- streamlined ornament
- sharp geometric border

## Prompt Formula

```text
[format] for [subject], Art Deco-inspired graphic design, symmetrical composition, stepped geometric ornament, sunburst motifs, black and deep jewel tones with metallic gold accents, elegant vertical layout, sharp decorative border, vintage luxury poster feel, no photorealism, no clutter, no cartoon style
```
