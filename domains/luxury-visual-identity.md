# Luxury Visual Identity

## Direction

Luxury visual identity relies on restraint, material quality, composition, and silence. It should avoid noisy decoration and cheap shine.

## Visual Traits

- quiet confidence
- refined negative space
- tactile materials
- controlled contrast
- precise typography zones
- minimal but rich detail
- premium photography or render finish
- coherent color restraint

## Material Cues

```text
brushed metal, polished stone, matte ceramic, soft leather, frosted glass, embossed paper, silk texture, controlled gold foil
```

## Lighting

```text
soft directional studio light, subtle specular highlights, controlled reflections, deep shadow separation, premium editorial lighting
```

## Prompt Formula

```text
luxury visual identity key visual for [brand/product], quiet premium tone, refined negative space, tactile material detail, controlled studio lighting, elegant composition, restrained palette, precise typography area, editorial-grade finish, no cheap gold shine, no clutter, no generic luxury template, no overdecorated background
```
