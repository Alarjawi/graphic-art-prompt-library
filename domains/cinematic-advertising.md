# Cinematic Advertising

## Direction

Cinematic advertising should feel like a crafted frame from a high-end commercial. It needs story, atmosphere, subject intention, camera logic, and controlled lighting.

## Visual Traits

- story-driven frame
- believable human action
- strong camera placement
- foreground depth cues
- natural but intentional lighting
- atmospheric separation
- premium grade color
- production design coherence

## Camera Language

```text
35mm environmental frame, 50mm natural perspective, 85mm premium portrait compression, low-angle heroic frame, slow commercial film still, shallow depth of field
```

## Lighting Language

```text
soft key light, warm practical lights, golden-hour rim light, volumetric atmosphere, controlled contrast, premium commercial color grade
```

## Prompt Formula

```text
cinematic advertising frame for [campaign/product], believable story moment, premium commercial film still, [lens] camera language, controlled composition, atmospheric depth, intentional lighting, refined color grade, realistic people and environment, no AI plastic skin, no overprocessed HDR, no random text, no generic stock-photo pose
```
