# Saudi Government Campaigns

## Direction

Saudi government campaign imagery should feel credible, premium, clear, culturally accurate, and future-facing. The tone is confident and public-service oriented, not decorative or exaggerated.

## Visual Traits

- clean institutional authority
- Saudi urban and architectural accuracy
- real public-service context
- warm but disciplined human presence
- national transformation tone
- premium but not luxury-fashion coded
- clear hierarchy for campaign messaging
- cinematic realism with restrained polish

## Useful Context Cues

```text
Riyadh urban context, Saudi institutional architecture, public service environment, Vision 2030 transformation tone, contemporary Saudi citizens, premium civic campaign, clean governmental visual language
```

## Composition

```text
single strong hero subject, architectural depth, clean negative space for headline, controlled foreground detail, balanced national scale and human detail
```

## Color Logic

```text
deep green accents, warm stone neutrals, bright daylight whites, restrained gold, modern civic blue, clean black typography zones
```

## Prompt Formula

```text
premium Saudi government campaign key visual for [service/topic], confident civic tone, contemporary Saudi environment, culturally accurate people and architecture, cinematic but realistic photography, clear hero subject, clean headline space, disciplined visual hierarchy, warm daylight with controlled contrast, premium public-sector finish, no fake Arabic text, no random logos, no generic stock-photo look, no inaccurate clothing or architecture
```
