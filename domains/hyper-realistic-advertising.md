# Hyper Realistic Advertising

## Direction

Hyper-realistic advertising should look like a high-budget production, not a raw photo. The goal is realism plus art direction.

## Visual Traits

- precise material behavior
- realistic scale
- controlled imperfections
- believable reflections
- product-safe detail
- production-grade lighting
- clean commercial finish
- no surreal accidents

## Production Cues

```text
high-end commercial photography, product-safe lighting, controlled reflection cards, realistic contact shadows, natural lens behavior, premium post-production grade
```

## Prompt Formula

```text
hyper-realistic advertising image for [product/service], high-budget commercial production, precise material realism, believable reflections and shadows, controlled studio or location lighting, natural lens behavior, premium post-production finish, crisp focal hierarchy, no CGI plastic look, no warped objects, no fake text, no unrealistic scale, no over-sharpened HDR
```
