# Prompt Quality Checklist

Use this before generating final prompts.

## Strategy

- Is the campaign goal clear?
- Is the audience or context clear?
- Does the prompt have one dominant idea?

## Art Direction

- Is there a visual world?
- Is the composition controlled?
- Is the hierarchy clear?
- Is there space for headline or logo if needed?

## Saudi Accuracy

- Is the environment culturally and architecturally believable?
- Are people, clothing, gestures, and public spaces accurate?
- Are Arabic text and logos avoided unless supplied?

## Production Quality

- Are lens and lighting cues specific?
- Are materials and textures believable?
- Is the image realistic without looking generic?

## Negative Controls

- Does the prompt block stock-photo output?
- Does it prevent fake Arabic text?
- Does it prevent random logos?
- Does it prevent plastic skin, warped hands, and wrong scale?

## Final Test

The prompt should read like a short creative brief, not a bag of keywords.
